My Story

Foot Steps

  • Began my educational journey at UNG Per School (age 5-6).
  • Attended Homagama Maha Vidyalaya from grade 1 to 5.
  • Successfully passed the Scholarship Exam and became eligible for high school at Colombo D S Senanayake College - Colombo 07.
  • Completed Ordinary Level (OL) exams at D S Senanayake College - Colombo 07 with grades A8's and B1.
  • Achieved success in the General Certificate of Education Advanced Level (GCE AL) exams with S3 in Combined Maths, Chemistry, and Physics, and C1 in General English.
  • Enrolled in a Web Programming Course at Kelaniya University and achieved a Grade A+ result.
  • Started working as an Associate Image processor (AIP) at Orel IT in Navinna, primarily focusing on data entry.
  • Commenced an English Diploma at Aquinas College in Colombo but dropped out due to lack of interest.
  • Sparked an interest in programming through IOT programming using an Arduino Board obtained from a friend.
  • Embarked on a Mechatronics Engineering Degree at Open University in Sri Lanka.Unfortunately, dropped out.
  • Joined DataIntimate (pvt) Ltd, a startup business, as a Web Developer.
  • Left my position at Orel IT.
  • Recognized the need to learn programming for developing mobile and web applications for my e-commerce venture.
  • Enrolled in a Graduate Diploma program at IJSE.
  • Started an internship as a Software Engineer Intern at Epic Lanka PVT Ltd.
  • Successfully graduated from IJSE.
  • Left my job at Epic Lanka.
  • Rejoined Epic Lanka by signing a contract to work for an extended period.
  • Embarked on a Computer Science degree (BSc Hons) in Software Engineering at Kingston University London.
  • Started working full-time at Data Intimate PVT Ltd as a Software Engineer and assumed the role of Chief Technology Officer.
  • Joined IJSE as a Visiting Lecturer, sharing my knowledge and experience with aspiring software engineers.
  • Graduated from Kingston University.
